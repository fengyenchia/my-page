Hello, I am Yen-Chia Feng. I am currently a student at the Department of Advertising at National Chengchi University, with a double major in the Bachelor's Program in Digital Content and Technology. I specialize in creating visual works with clean lines and vibrant colors, and I also enjoy teamwork and the process of mutual collaboration.

During my time at university, I have broadened my horizons through internships and club activities. As an assistant at the Digital Platform of NCCU's College of Communication, I also served as a teaching team leader, responsible for organizing design and photography workshops. This experience allowed me to develop skills in event planning and communication. Additionally, I served as the art director for the drama club, where I applied my design skills to create posters. I am currently taking on freelance projects, applying what I have learned to practical work.

In my free time, I enjoy drawing and photography to document the details of daily life, which also serves as a source of design inspiration. I firmly believe that continuous learning and hands-on practice are the most effective ways to improve myself. In the future, I hope to continue my passion and enhance my skills to create more interesting and meaningful works.

For me, design inspiration comes from daily life. I love combining design with technology, using coding and digital tools to create new possibilities for art and creativity.

>  Daily Life is the wellspring of my design inspiration.
