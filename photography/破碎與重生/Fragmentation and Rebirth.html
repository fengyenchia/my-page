Rebirth and Fragmentation

This series explores the profound beauty found in transformation. It begins with the concept of fragmentation, not as an end, but as a crucial point of disruption. The act of breaking apart—whether of a physical object, an idea, or an emotion—is seen as the catalyst for change. Yet, it is within these fractured moments that true rebirth occurs. We are invited to look past the initial damage and witness how these shattered pieces can be reconfigured, finding new form, new purpose, and a new, more resilient beauty. This work celebrates the strength in imperfection and the profound journey from brokenness to a reconstructed whole.
本系列探索在轉變中所發現的深刻之美。它始於「破碎」這個概念，但這並非終點，而是一個關鍵的轉折點。分離的行為—無論是實體物件、一個想法或一種情感—被視為變革的催化劑。然而，正是在這些破碎的時刻中，真正的重生得以發生。我們受邀超越最初的損傷，去見證這些破碎的片段如何被重新組建，尋找到新的形態、新的用途，以及一種嶄新且更具韌性的美。這項作品頌揚了不完美中的力量，以及從破損走向重構完整的深刻旅程。

Once-discarded objects have been carefully arranged, turning a potential hazard into a fascinating, striking, and permanent piece of art.
曾經被丟棄之物已被精心安置，將潛在的危險轉化為一種迷人、引人注目且恆久的藝術品。
