COMIC STRIPT

The story of a jaded steamed bun who, wanting only to be left alone, unexpectedly embarks on an adventure to rescue his friends and becomes a cool hero.
一顆只想獨處的厭世包子，意外地踏上了一場救援朋友的冒險旅程，並成為了一個超酷的英雄。

"The Extraordinary Story of this Jaded Steamed Bun" masterfully tells the tale of its protagonist, "Xiao Bu"—a world-weary steamed bun whose only desire is to sleep peacefully in his steamer basket. His quiet existence is shattered when a group of naive and enthusiastic food friends begs for his help. The story follows his journey from immense reluctance to becoming the unlikely leader of a mission to rescue a friend trapped in the perilous depths of a refrigerator. This is more than just a thrilling and humorous adventure; it's a profound commentary on one's outlook on life.
Facing the inevitable fate of being eaten, should one embrace cynicism or seize the day?Through its charming and moving narrative, the comic celebrates the power of friendship and proves that a true hero can emerge from the most ordinary moments.
《這顆厭世包子的不平凡故事》深刻描繪了主角「小不」—— 一顆看透世事、只想在蒸籠裡獨自安睡的包子。當一群天真熱情的食物夥伴們前來求救時，他原本的寧靜被徹底打破。故事圍繞著他如何從極度不情願，到運用智慧帶領大家撬開冰箱、深入險境，最終成功救出同伴。這不僅是一場充滿趣味與緊張的冒險，更是一場關於生命態度的深刻探討：
在被食用的最終宿命下，是該選擇消極厭世，還是擁抱當下、及時行樂？ 漫畫透過幽默與感人的情節，讚頌了友誼的珍貴，並證明了真正的英雄，往往誕生於最平凡的時刻。
